About

DAV Institute of Engineering & Technology Jalandhar got established in year 2001 under the aegis of DAV college Managing committee. The institute is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Punjab Technical University,Jalandhar. This is the first degree level Engineering Institute started by the DAV College Trust and Management Society.The DAV College Trust and Management Society is the largest non-government educational organization in India managing a chain of 700 institutions in India and abroad.
